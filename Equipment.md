SRT Gear List

* hand ascender 
* chest ascender 
* foot ascender
* 6x screw gate carabiners (already have 4)
* 3-4m length of static 8-10mm rope, 
* Caving harness
* Braking descender which does not twist rope
  * [Petzl Stop](https://www.aspiring.co.nz/product/petzl-stop-descender/)
